Astrolabe Photography

My name is Victor Mendes and I created Astrolabe Photography. My work is a reflection of what I love, travel photography, landscape, fine art, bodyscapes and astrophotography. The astrolabe was used as a tool to calculate the position of celestial bodies and for navigators to find their way. Like the astrolabe, photography helps me find my path, discover myself and share my journey.


Astrolabe Photography's objective is to create content, whether you're just looking for a beautiful photo or you need to commission a project to fit your specific needs. I'm available for hire and I would love to hear about you and your project.
